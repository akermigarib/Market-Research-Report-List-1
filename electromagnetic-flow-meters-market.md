<p><p>Electromagnetic flow meters are instruments used to measure the flow of conductive fluids, and they primarily come in two types: Direct-Current (DC) type and Induction type. The DC type utilizes a constant magnetic field and measures voltage induced by flow, making it suitable for low flow rates. In contrast, the Induction type uses alternating magnetic fields, allowing for greater accuracy and versatility across a wider range of flow conditions. Both types cater to various industrial applications, ensuring effective fluid management.</p></p>
