<p><strong>The Automotive SiC Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SiC MOSFET</li><li>SiC-SBD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive SiC (Silicon Carbide) technology market comprises mainly two types: SiC MOSFETs (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistors) and SiC-SBDs (Schottky Barrier Diodes). SiC MOSFETs are favored for their high efficiency and thermal performance, making them ideal for electric vehicle powertrains and energy conversion systems. In contrast, SiC-SBDs are utilized for their rapid switching capabilities and reduced losses in high-frequency applications, enhancing overall system efficiency. Together, these components drive advancements in electric mobility and renewable energy applications.</p></p>
