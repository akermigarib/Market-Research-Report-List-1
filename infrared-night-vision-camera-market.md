<p><p>The infrared night vision camera market comprises three main types: Thermal Imaging, Near Infrared Imaging, and Low Light Imaging. Thermal Imaging captures heat signatures, enabling visibility in total darkness by detecting temperature differences. Near Infrared Imaging utilizes near-infrared light to enhance visibility in low-light conditions, ideal for surveillance and security. Low Light Imaging amplifies available light, allowing for clear imaging in dim environments. Each technology serves distinct applications across military, security, and wildlife monitoring sectors, enhancing situational awareness in low-visibility scenarios.</p></p>
