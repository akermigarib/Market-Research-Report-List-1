<p><strong>The Dental Casting Porcelain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Fusing</li><li>Medium Fusing</li><li>Low Fusing</li><li>Ultra-Low Fusing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental casting porcelain market is segmented into four main types based on fusing temperature. High-fusing porcelain is used for metal-ceramic restorations, ensuring durability under high temperatures. Medium-fusing porcelain offers a balance between strength and aesthetic appeal, suitable for various dental applications. Low-fusing porcelain melts at lower temperatures, allowing for better adhesion to metals while maintaining translucency. Ultra-low fusing porcelain, with the lowest melting point, is typically employed for esthetic restorations and minimal thermal stress on underlying structures.</p></p>
