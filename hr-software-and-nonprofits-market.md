<p><p>HR software for nonprofits typically encompasses three key market types: HRIS, HRMS, and HCM. HRIS (Human Resource Information System) focuses on managing employee data and record-keeping. HRMS (Human Resource Management System) extends this functionality by integrating payroll, benefits, and recruitment processes. HCM (Human Capital Management) takes a broader approach, emphasizing strategic workforce planning, talent management, and employee development. These systems help nonprofits streamline operations, enhance efficiency, and optimize human resources to better serve their missions.</p></p>
