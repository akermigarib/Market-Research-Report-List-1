<p><p>Worm geared motors are categorized into two main types based on their design and operational characteristics: helical-worm and double-worm. Helical-worm geared motors combine helical gears with worm gears, offering increased efficiency and reduced backlash, making them suitable for high torque applications. Meanwhile, double-worm geared motors feature two worm gears, enhancing torque capacity and providing better load distribution, leading to improved durability. Both types cater to various industrial applications requiring reliable motion control and precision.</p></p>
