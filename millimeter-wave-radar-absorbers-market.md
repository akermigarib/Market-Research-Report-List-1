<p><p>Millimeter wave radar absorbers are specialized materials designed to minimize radar signal reflection. The market is primarily categorized into flat absorbing materials and pyramidal absorbing materials. Flat absorbing materials offer a compact solution suitable for applications where space is limited, providing effective radar absorption across various frequencies. Pyramidal absorbing materials, characterized by their geometric shape, enhance signal attenuation and performance by trapping waves at various angles, making them ideal for shielding and testing environments. Both types are crucial for stealth technology and electronic warfare.</p></p>
