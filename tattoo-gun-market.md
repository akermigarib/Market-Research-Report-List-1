<p><p>The tattoo gun market features three main types: electromagnetic, motor, and pneumatic tattoo guns. Electromagnetic tattoo guns use electromagnetic coils to drive the needle, offering precision and versatility. Motor tattoo guns utilize small motors for a smoother operation, providing consistent needle movement and reduced vibration, making them popular for long sessions. Pneumatic tattoo guns operate using compressed air, allowing for lighter weight and less noise, appealing to artists seeking comfort and efficiency. Each type caters to specific artist preferences and needs.</p></p>
