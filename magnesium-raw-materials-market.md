<p><p>The Magnesium Raw Materials market includes two primary types: Phanerocrystalline Magnesite and Cryptocrystalline Magnesite. Phanerocrystalline Magnesite features a coarse crystalline structure and is often utilized in the production of refractory materials and magnesium compounds. In contrast, Cryptocrystalline Magnesite has a fine-grained structure, making it suitable for applications requiring high purity and finer particle sizes, such as in the production of magnesium oxide. Both types play crucial roles in various industrial applications, including construction and manufacturing.</p></p>
