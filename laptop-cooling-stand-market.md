<p><p>The laptop cooling stand market comprises two main types: active cooling stands and passive cooling stands. Active cooling stands feature built-in fans that promote airflow, effectively dissipating heat for enhanced performance during resource-intensive tasks. In contrast, passive cooling stands rely on materials and design to naturally disperse heat without mechanical components, providing a quieter option. Both types aim to prolong laptop lifespan and maintain optimal performance, catering to different user preferences and needs in cooling solutions.</p></p>
