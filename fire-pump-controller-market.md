<p><p>The fire pump controller market is primarily categorized into two types: diesel-driven and electric-driven fire pump controllers. Diesel-driven fire pump controllers are powered by internal combustion engines, making them suitable for applications where a reliable power supply is crucial, including remote locations. In contrast, electric-driven fire pump controllers rely on electricity for operation, offering quieter performance and lower emissions, ideal for urban environments. Both types are essential for ensuring effective fire suppression in various settings.</p></p>
