<p><p>A drain camera, also known as a sewer inspection camera, is a specialized tool used to visually inspect the interior of drains, sewers, and pipelines. The growing emphasis on infrastructure maintenance and the rise in plumbing services have catalyzed the demand for drain cameras in both commercial and residential sectors.</p><p>The Drain Camera Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.2% during the forecast period. Emerging trends include the development of compact and portable models, enhancing usability for field technicians. Moreover, the trend towards sustainability and eco-friendly solutions is encouraging innovations in inspection technologies, making drain cameras essential tools for efficient plumbing maintenance and infrastructure management.
