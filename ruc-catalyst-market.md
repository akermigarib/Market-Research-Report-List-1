<p><p>The Ru/C catalyst market is divided into two main types: non-homogeneous and homogeneous Ru/C catalysts. Non-homogeneous Ru/C catalysts typically consist of solid-phase ruthenium supported on carbon, allowing for easy separation from reaction mixtures, making them suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, homogeneous Ru/C catalysts are soluble in the reaction medium, offering uniform reactivity and potentially higher selectivity in chemical processes. Each type serves distinct markets, catering to specific needs in catalysis, pharmaceuticals, and fine chemical production.</p></p>
