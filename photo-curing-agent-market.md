<p><ul><li>Free-radical Type</li><li>Cationic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photo curing agent market is segmented into two main types: free-radical and cationic. Free-radical photo curing agents initiate polymerization through free radicals generated by light exposure, making them suitable for various applications like coatings and adhesives. In contrast, cationic photo curing agents use cationic polymerization, providing advantages such as superior chemical resistance and adhesion properties. Each type serves distinct applications depending on the desired material properties, curing speed, and environmental considerations in industries like automotive, electronics, and packaging.</p></p>
