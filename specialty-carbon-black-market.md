<p><p>The specialty carbon black market comprises various types, including Lamp Black, Acetylene Black, Gas Black, and Others. Lamp Black, created by incomplete combustion, is primarily used in inks and coatings. Acetylene Black, derived from acetylene gas, offers high conductivity and is favored in battery applications. Gas Black is produced from hydrocarbon gases and is commonly utilized in tires and industrial rubber products. The "Others" category encompasses specialty grades used in diverse applications like plastics, paints, and electronics.</p></p>
