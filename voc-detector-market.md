<p><strong>The VOC Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC (Volatile Organic Compound) detector market primarily comprises two key types: Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID detectors use ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules, providing rapid and sensitive detection of a wide range of VOCs, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, MOS sensors operate by changing their electrical resistance in the presence of VOCs, offering a cost-effective solution for indoor air quality monitoring and environmental safety. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring health and safety.</p></p>
