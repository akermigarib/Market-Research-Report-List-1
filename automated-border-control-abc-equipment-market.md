<p><p>Automated Border Control (ABC) equipment includes two primary market types: ABC e-gates and ABC kiosks. ABC e-gates are streamlined entry points that employ biometrics, such as facial recognition or fingerprint scans, to verify travelers' identities, enhancing security and efficiency at border crossings. ABC kiosks, on the other hand, are self-service terminals that allow travelers to check in, scan documents, and complete customs declarations independently, reducing wait times and improving the flow of passengers at airports and border facilities.</p></p>
