<p><p>The commercial gas turbines market is primarily categorized into two types: single-axis and dual-axis turbines. Single-axis gas turbines operate around a single rotational axis, offering simplicity and efficiency for straightforward applications. In contrast, dual-axis gas turbines feature two axes of rotation, enabling enhanced flexibility and performance, particularly in complex or variable conditions. These configurations cater to different energy needs, with single-axis turbines typically used in standard power generation, while dual-axis designs excel in specialized applications requiring adaptability and greater output.</p></p>
