<p><ul><li>Balanced Armature Drivers</li><li>Dynamic Drivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-ear monitor (IEM) drivers are crucial for audio performance, primarily divided into balanced armature and dynamic drivers. Balanced armature drivers offer precise sound reproduction and are compact, making them ideal for detailed listening. They typically handle mids and highs well but may lack in bass response. Dynamic drivers, on the other hand, use a diaphragm and are known for their ability to produce deep bass and a more natural sound. Both driver types cater to varying preferences in audio quality and listening experiences.</p></p>
