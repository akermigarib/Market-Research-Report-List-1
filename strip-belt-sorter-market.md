<p><p>The Strip Belt Sorter market is segmented based on angular configurations: 30°, 45°, and 90°. Each type determines how materials are diverted to specific locations. The 30° sorter is ideal for moderate incline applications, allowing efficient sorting without excessive space usage. The 45° sorter offers a balance between performance and space, suitable for various sorting tasks. The 90° sorter is designed for maximal directional change, optimizing space and enhancing sorting efficiency in environments with limited area and high throughput demands.</p></p>
