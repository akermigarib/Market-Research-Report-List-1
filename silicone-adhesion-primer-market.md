<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone adhesion primers are designed to enhance the bonding of silicone sealants and coatings to various surfaces. In the market, they are primarily categorized into two types: water-based and solvent-based primers. Water-based primers are eco-friendly, easy to clean, and have low odor, making them suitable for indoor use. In contrast, solvent-based primers offer stronger adhesion and are often used in challenging outdoor environments due to their durability and resistance to moisture, though they may emit stronger fumes.</p></p>
