<p><p>The Ceramic Bonding Tool market includes two main types: capillaries and wedge bonding tools. Capillaries are used for precision wire bonding in electronics, facilitating a strong connection between semiconductor components. These tools feature a narrow opening to deliver solder or bonding materials efficiently. Wedge bonding tools, on the other hand, utilize a flat tip to create ultrasonic bonds between materials, offering a reliable method for joining metals and semiconductor devices. Both types are essential for enhancing device performance in various applications.</p></p>
