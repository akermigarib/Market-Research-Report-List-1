<p><ul><li>Surface Resistivity Meter</li><li>Volume Resistivity Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The analog resistivity meters market comprises two main types: surface resistivity meters and volume resistivity meters. Surface resistivity meters measure the resistance of a material's surface, providing insights into its conductivity and suitability for various applications, especially in concrete and asphalt testing. Volume resistivity meters, on the other hand, assess the overall resistivity of a material by evaluating its internal structure, making them ideal for analyzing the electrical properties of soils and other substrates. Both types are essential for geophysical surveys and quality control.</p></p>
