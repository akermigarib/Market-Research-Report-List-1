<p><p>The touch-free thermometer market consists of three main types: Single Mode, Dual Mode, and Multi Mode. Single Mode thermometers measure temperature using only one method, typically from the forehead. Dual Mode devices can measure temperature both from the forehead and the ear, offering versatility for different age groups and situations. Multi Mode thermometers combine various measurement methods, including forehead, ear, and sometimes oral or rectal options, providing comprehensive functionality for accurate temperature assessments in diverse settings.</p></p>
