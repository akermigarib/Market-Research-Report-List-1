<p><p>The Transmission Fiber Patchcord market consists of two primary types: Single Mode Fiber (SMF) Patchcord and Multimode Fiber (MMF) Patchcord. Single Mode Fiber Patchcords use a single light path, making them ideal for long-distance communication with high bandwidth and minimal signal loss. In contrast, Multimode Fiber Patchcords utilize multiple light paths, suitable for short-distance applications, offering lower cost and installation flexibility. Both types serve essential roles in telecommunications, data centers, and networking systems, catering to diverse connectivity needs.</p></p>
