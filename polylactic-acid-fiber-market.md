<p><p>The Polylactic Acid (PLA) fiber market can be segmented into two primary types: monomer preparation and polymerization preparation. Monomer preparation involves the initial steps of producing lactic acid, derived from renewable resources like corn or sugarcane. This lactic acid is then utilized in the polymerization process, where it is converted into polylactic acid, resulting in biodegradable fiber materials. These fibers are increasingly sought after for their sustainable properties and applications in textiles, packaging, and other industries, aligning with eco-friendly initiatives.</p></p>
