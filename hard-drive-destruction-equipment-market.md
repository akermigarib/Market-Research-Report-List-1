<p><p>The hard drive destruction equipment market includes various degaussing technologies designed to securely erase data from magnetic storage devices. Coil degaussers utilize electromagnetic coils to disrupt magnetic fields and erase data. Capacitive discharge degaussers release stored electrical energy in a quick burst, effectively wiping data through intense magnetic fields. Permanent magnet degaussers use strong permanent magnets to create a constant magnetic flux that erases information on hard drives. Each type offers unique advantages in data destruction efficiency and applications.</p></p>
