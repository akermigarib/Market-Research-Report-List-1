<p><p>Biogas power plants can be categorized based on their feedstock sources. Industry wastewater treatment plants harness organic materials from industrial effluents, contributing to energy recovery and waste reduction. Municipal sewage plants process human waste to generate biogas, transforming public waste into a valuable energy resource. Lastly, landfills capture methane emissions from decomposing organic waste, converting a greenhouse gas into usable energy and mitigating environmental impact.</p></p>
