<p><strong>The Fabric Relaxing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forward Rotation</li><li>Forward and Reverse Rotation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fabric Relaxing Machine market primarily features two types: Forward Rotation and Forward and Reverse Rotation. Forward Rotation machines are designed to unwind and relax fabric by advancing it in a single direction, effectively reducing tension and creases. In contrast, Forward and Reverse Rotation machines offer more versatility, allowing the fabric to move both forward and backward. This dual movement helps achieve a more uniform relaxation and can handle various fabric types, catering to different production requirements.</p></p>
