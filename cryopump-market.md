<p><p>A cryopump is a vacuum pump that operates at cryogenic temperatures to remove gases from a vacuum chamber. It utilizes the principle of cryogenic condensation and adsorption to capture molecules, effectively creating high vacuum conditions. Widely used in various industries, including semiconductor manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and space technology, cryopumps are essential for processes that require ultra-high vacuum conditions.</p><p>The Cryopump Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period.
