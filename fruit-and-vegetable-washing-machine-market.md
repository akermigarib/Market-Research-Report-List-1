<p><p>The fruit and vegetable washing machine market features two prominent types: ultrasonic and ozone-based systems. Ultrasonic washing machines use high-frequency sound waves to create microbubbles that penetrate dirt and contaminants, ensuring thorough cleaning without damaging produce. In contrast, ozone washing machines utilize ozone gas, known for its strong oxidizing properties, to effectively disinfect and eliminate pesticides and bacteria. Both technologies enhance food safety and prolong shelf life, catering to the growing demand for hygienic food processing solutions.</p></p>
