<p><p>The Idle Stop Start Batteries market is primarily categorized into AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) and EFB (Enhanced Flooded Battery) types. AGM batteries are designed for frequent cycling and quick recharging, making them ideal for stop-start systems in vehicles. They offer better performance in extreme temperatures and longer lifespan. EFB batteries, on the other hand, are cost-effective alternatives that provide improved capacity and durability over traditional flooded batteries, suited for moderate stop-start applications. Both types support fuel efficiency and reduced emissions in modern vehicles.</p></p>
