<p><p>The Electrostatic Chuck System market comprises two main types: Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks. Coulomb Type utilizes electrostatic forces to hold substrates in place, benefiting applications requiring high holding power and precision. In contrast, JR Type employs a combination of electrostatic and mechanical forces, offering superior stability and ease of use. Both systems are vital in semiconductor manufacturing, enhancing process efficiency and enabling advanced production technologies.</p></p>
