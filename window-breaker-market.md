<p><strong>The Window Breaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spring Window Breaker</li><li>Static Punching Window Breaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Window breaker markets are categorized primarily into two types: Spring Window Breakers and Static Punching Window Breakers. Spring Window Breakers utilize a spring mechanism to deliver a powerful strike, facilitating rapid window shattering. In contrast, Static Punching Window Breakers rely on brute force, using solid materials to puncture or break glass without any moving parts. Both types cater to various needs, such as security, emergency egress, or vehicle rescue, with each offering distinct advantages in terms of speed and effectiveness.</p></p>
