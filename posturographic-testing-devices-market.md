Technological advancements such as improved sensor technology and integration with telehealth platforms are also enhancing the functionality and appeal of these devices.</p><p>Additionally, the growing trend of preventive healthcare and fitness monitoring is driving demand for posturographic assessments in clinical and wellness settings.
