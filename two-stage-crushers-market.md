<p><p>The two-stage crushers market encompasses various types designed for efficient material processing. Jaw crushers break down large rocks into smaller pieces, while impact crushers utilize high-speed impacts for size reduction. Cone crushers crush materials through a combination of compression and shear forces, ideal for producing uniform granules.
