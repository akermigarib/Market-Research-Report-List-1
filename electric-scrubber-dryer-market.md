<p><p>The electric scrubber dryer market comprises two main types: walk-behind scrubber dryers and stand-on/ride-on scrubber dryers. Walk-behind scrubber dryers are compact, maneuverable machines ideal for smaller spaces, allowing users to scrub and dry floors while physically walking alongside them. In contrast, stand-on or ride-on scrubber dryers are larger, enabling operators to stand or sit while driving the machine, making them suitable for expansive areas. Both types enhance floor cleaning efficiency and are used in various commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
