<p><strong>The Furniture Wood Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Waterborne</li><li>Radiation-cured</li><li>Powder</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The furniture wood coatings market comprises several types, including solvent-borne, waterborne, radiation-cured, and powder coatings. Solvent-borne coatings offer excellent durability and finish but may emit volatile organic compounds. Waterborne coatings are eco-friendly, low in VOCs, and easy to clean, making them popular for residential use. Radiation-cured coatings, such as UV-cured, harden quickly under light exposure, providing efficient production benefits. Powder coatings are applied electrostatically and cured through heat, resulting in a durable finish. Other market types may include hybrid formulations.</p></p>
