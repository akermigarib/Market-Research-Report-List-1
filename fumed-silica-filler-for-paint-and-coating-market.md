<p><p>Fumed silica filler is a highly versatile material used in the paint and coating industry to enhance texture, improve stability, and control rheology. Made through the flame hydrolysis of silicon tetrachloride, it consists of small, non-aggregated particles that provide unique properties such as high surface area and low density. These characteristics make fumed silica an effective thickening agent, modifying the viscosity, improving suspension stability, and preventing sagging in paints and coatings.</p><p>The market for fumed silica fillers in paint and coatings is poised for steady growth. The global push toward high-performance and environmentally friendly products has driven the demand for advanced coatings, which is beneficial for fumed silica applications.
