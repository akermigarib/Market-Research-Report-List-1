<p><strong>The Double Fired Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Atmosphere</li><li>Vacuum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The double-fired furnace market is divided into two main types: atmospheric and vacuum. Atmospheric furnaces operate under normal atmospheric pressure and are commonly used for processes like annealing, sintering, and heat treating. They allow for efficient material processing with controlled environments. Vacuum furnaces, on the other hand, operate under low-pressure conditions, minimizing oxidation and enhancing material quality. This type is ideal for applications requiring high purity and precision, such as aerospace and medical industries, where material integrity is paramount.</p></p>
