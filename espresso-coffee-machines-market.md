<p><p>Espresso coffee machines are categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require the barista to control all brewing parameters, offering a traditional and hands-on experience. Semi-automatic machines provide some automation, controlling water flow while allowing users to manage grind and tamping. Fully automatic machines handle the entire brewing process with minimal user involvement, featuring programmable settings for convenience. Each type caters to different preferences, from craftsmanship to ease of use.</p></p>
