<p><p>The rugged power supply market encompasses AC-DC and DC-DC power supplies designed to operate in harsh environments. AC-DC power supplies convert alternating current to direct current, powering equipment that requires stable voltage levels. They are often used in industrial and military applications. DC-DC power supplies, on the other hand, take an input DC voltage and convert it to a different DC voltage level, ideal for powering various electronic devices in demanding settings. Both types ensure reliability and durability in extreme conditions.</p></p>
