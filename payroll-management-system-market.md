<p><p>Payroll Management Systems can be categorized into three market types: On-premise, Software-as-a-Service (SaaS), and Cloud-hosted solutions. On-premise systems are installed locally on company servers, offering control but requiring significant IT resources. SaaS solutions are subscription-based services accessed online, providing automatic updates and lower upfront costs. Cloud-hosted systems combine elements of both, offering flexibility and scalability while maintaining secure data storage offsite. Each type caters to different organizational needs, budgets, and IT capabilities.</p></p>
