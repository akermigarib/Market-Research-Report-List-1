<p><p>The cardiac monitoring products market includes several types, primarily focused on tracking heart activity. Resting ECG (Electrocardiogram) provides a snapshot of heart function while the patient is at rest. Stress ECG monitors heart performance during physical exertion. Holter ECG offers continuous monitoring over 24-48 hours, capturing heart rhythms in daily activities. Other types encompass various innovative devices and technologies, such as event monitors and telemetry systems, catering to diverse patient needs and enhancing cardiac diagnosis and management.</p></p>
