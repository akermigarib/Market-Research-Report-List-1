<p><p>The waterproof material market consists of various types, primarily including waterproofing membranes, waterproofing agents, and others. Waterproofing membranes are barrier materials used to prevent water infiltration in structures, often made from materials like PVC, TPO, or EPDM. Waterproofing agents are chemical solutions applied to surfaces to enhance their water resistance, suitable for concrete, wood, or fabric. The "others" category encompasses ancillary products and technologies, such as coatings and sealants, that also contribute to effective waterproofing solutions in various applications.</p></p>
