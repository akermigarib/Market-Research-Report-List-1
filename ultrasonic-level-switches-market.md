<p><p>Ultrasonic level switches are essential instruments used to detect liquid levels in various applications. The market can be segmented into externally mounted and non-externally mounted types. Externally mounted switches utilize ultrasonic sensors positioned outside the vessel or tank, allowing for non-contact measurement, which reduces the risk of contamination. In contrast, non-externally mounted switches are installed directly inside the container, providing accurate readings but may encounter issues with buildup or maintenance. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
