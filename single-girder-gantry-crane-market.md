<p><p>The single girder gantry crane market consists of various types, primarily the gantry crane and half-gantry crane. A gantry crane features a bridge supported by two or more vertical legs, typically used for heavy lifting in large outdoor areas. In contrast, a half-gantry crane has one leg that rests on the ground while the other is mounted on an elevated track, making it ideal for limited spaces. Both designs are crucial in construction, shipping, and manufacturing sectors for efficient material handling.</p></p>
