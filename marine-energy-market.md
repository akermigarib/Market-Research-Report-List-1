<p><p>The marine energy market encompasses various technologies harnessing oceanic forces. Ocean thermal energy utilizes temperature differences between warm surface water and colder deep water to generate energy. Additional markets include salinity gradient energy, which harnesses energy from saltwater and freshwater mixing, showcasing the diverse potential of marine energy sources.</p></p>
