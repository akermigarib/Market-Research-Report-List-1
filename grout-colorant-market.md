<p><p>The grout colorant market features two primary types: epoxy grout and stain grout colorants. Epoxy grout is a durable, water-resistant option, making it ideal for high-moisture areas like kitchens and bathrooms. It offers excellent adhesion and color retention. Stain grout colorants, on the other hand, are designed to revitalize existing grout lines by penetrating and changing their color without the need for full replacement. Both types cater to consumers seeking aesthetic enhancements and maintenance solutions for tiled surfaces.</p></p>
