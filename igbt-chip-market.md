<p><p>The IGBT chip market encompasses three primary applications: discrete IGBTs, IGBT modules, and IGBT Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs). Discrete IGBTs are used in low to medium power applications, ensuring efficient switching in devices like power supplies. IGBT modules integrate multiple IGBTs and drivers for higher power applications, ideal for motor drives and renewable energy systems. IGBT-IPMs combine control and power functions into a single unit, enhancing efficiency and compactness, commonly utilized in industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
