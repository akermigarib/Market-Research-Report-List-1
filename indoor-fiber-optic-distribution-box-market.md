<p><strong>The Indoor Fiber Optic Distribution Box Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Multi-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Indoor Fiber Optic Distribution Box market is primarily divided into two types: Single Mode and Multi-Mode fibers. Single Mode fibers use a single light source to transmit data over long distances with minimal attenuation, making them ideal for telecommunication networks and large institutional setups. Multi-Mode fibers, on the other hand, use multiple light paths and are suitable for shorter distances, commonly used in local area networks and data centers. Each type serves specific applications based on bandwidth and distance requirements.</p></p>
