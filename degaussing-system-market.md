<p><p>The degaussing system market can be divided into two primary types: external degaussing systems and shipborne degaussing systems. External degaussing systems are typically used for land-based applications, providing magnetic field reduction to protect sensitive equipment from magnetic interference. In contrast, shipborne degaussing systems are integrated into vessels to minimize magnetic signatures, enhancing maritime security and reducing vulnerability to naval mines or detection. Both types play critical roles in safeguarding technological integrity in various environments.</p></p>
