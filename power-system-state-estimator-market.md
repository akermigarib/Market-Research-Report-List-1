<p><p>In power system state estimation, the Weighted Least Squares (WLS) method minimizes the differences between observed and estimated values, applying weights to account for measurement reliability. The Interior Point (IP) method optimizes system performance using advanced mathematical techniques to find feasible solutions within defined boundaries, enhancing computational efficiency and convergence speed. Other methods may include the Recursive Estimator, Kalman Filter, and more, each tailored for specific scenarios to improve accuracy and speed in assessing system states under varying market conditions.</p></p>
